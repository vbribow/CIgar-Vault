# Document 013 — The Cedriva Platform Strategy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Draft  
**Owner:** Brian Bowers  
**Created:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

Cedriva is not an app.

Cedriva is a platform.

A platform becomes exponentially more valuable as more participants contribute.

The Cedriva Platform consists of six connected communities.

---

## Collectors

The heart of Cedriva.

They collect.

Review.

Learn.

Teach.

Preserve history.

Everything begins with them.

---

## Manufacturers

The creators.

They provide:

- Official releases
- Blend information
- Factory history
- Educational content
- Verification
- News
- Collector engagement

Cedriva becomes the official digital extension of their brands.

---

## Retailers

The educators.

They introduce new smokers to premium cigars.

Cedriva helps them:

- Educate customers
- Recommend cigars
- Track customer preferences
- Build loyalty
- Increase confidence
- Drive repeat business

Retailers grow because collectors become more knowledgeable.

---

## Lounges

The community centers.

Cedriva helps collectors discover lounges.

Lounges host events.

Collectors meet.

Knowledge spreads.

Culture grows.

---

## Content Creators

The storytellers.

Reviewers.

Podcasters.

YouTubers.

Photographers.

Historians.

Cedriva becomes the platform where their work lives forever.

---

## Industry Partners

Accessory manufacturers.

Humidor builders.

Travel companies.

Insurance providers.

Auction companies.

Charities.

Events.

Together they complete the ecosystem.

---

## When All Six Groups Contribute

Collectors gain knowledge.

Manufacturers gain loyal customers.

Retailers sell more cigars.

Lounges become stronger.

Creators reach larger audiences.

The industry grows.

Cedriva grows with it.

Everybody wins.
