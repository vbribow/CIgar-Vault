# Document 001 — The Cedriva Constitution

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Working Draft  
**Owner:** Brian Bowers  
**Created:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

> This document is the working source for Cedriva’s purpose, principles, commitments, and decision-making standards. It is intentionally unfinished and should evolve through deliberate review.

## Preamble

Cedriva exists to preserve, honor, and grow the culture of premium cigars—and to help every collection preserve the people, memories, milestones, and craftsmanship within it.

We are not simply building software. Every cigar reflects generations of farmers, blenders, rollers, factory teams, distributors, retailers, lounge owners, writers, historians, and collectors. Cedriva has a responsibility to strengthen that tradition for the generations that follow.

## Article I — Our Purpose

Cedriva will make the knowledge, tools, and community of premium cigar collecting more accessible without diminishing their depth, history, or meaning.

We will measure success by more than downloads, revenue, or collection valuations. We succeed when:

- Collectors know more and make better-informed decisions.
- New collectors feel welcomed into the culture.
- Experienced collectors can preserve and share their knowledge.
- Manufacturers and their products are represented accurately.
- Retailers, lounges, and other industry partners are strengthened.
- Cigar history and provenance are better preserved.
- Future generations discover and value the culture.

## Article II — The Four Governing Questions

Every material product, business, partnership, and community decision must answer four questions:

### 1. Does this educate?

We make expertise accessible without diminishing its depth.

### 2. Does this build trust?

We show sources, uncertainty, corrections, and the role of human judgment.

### 3. Does this strengthen the community?

We welcome new collectors and amplify experienced voices.

### 4. Does this preserve the culture?

We use technology to support craftsmanship, ritual, history, and industry partners.

If a decision cannot honestly satisfy all four questions, Cedriva will rethink it before proceeding.

## Article III — Our Commitments

### To new collectors

Every expert was once a beginner. Questions are welcomed, learning is celebrated, and every collector deserves to belong from day one.

### To experienced collectors

Experience deserves respect. Cedriva will amplify expertise and provide tools worthy of the world’s finest collections.

### To the premium cigar industry

Manufacturers, farmers, factory teams, distributors, retailers, lounges, media, historians, and collectors are partners in the same ecosystem. Cedriva succeeds only when that ecosystem succeeds.

### To truth

History, information, provenance, and authenticity matter. We will state uncertainty, identify sources, correct mistakes, and earn trust through honesty—not claims of perfection.

### To responsible innovation

Technology should remove barriers without replacing the rituals, relationships, craftsmanship, and traditions that make premium cigars meaningful.

## Article IV — Product and Technology Standards

Cedriva products should:

- Put the collector in control of their collection and records.
- Distinguish verified facts from estimates, opinions, and automated suggestions.
- Preserve useful source and provenance information.
- Explain consequential recommendations in understandable language.
- Protect collector privacy and collection security.
- Use artificial intelligence to assist human judgment, not impersonate certainty or expertise.
- Avoid features that weaken trust, exploit beginners, or reduce culture to transactions.

## Article V — Community Standards

Cedriva will foster a community built on education, generosity, respect, and shared stewardship.

Community participation should:

- Welcome sincere questions and constructive disagreement.
- Credit expertise and original sources.
- Respect differences in taste, budget, experience, and collecting style.
- Reject harassment, deception, counterfeit promotion, and predatory conduct.
- Keep education and cultural stewardship ahead of status seeking.

## Article VI — Business and Partnership Standards

Cedriva will pursue revenue in ways that support its purpose and preserve user trust.

Before entering a commercial relationship, Cedriva should determine:

- Whether the relationship is disclosed clearly.
- Whether editorial or product judgment remains independent.
- Whether collectors receive genuine value.
- Whether the partner strengthens the premium cigar ecosystem.
- Whether incentives could distort information, recommendations, or community trust.

## Article VII — Stewardship and Accountability

Cedriva will:

- Document material corrections and learn from mistakes.
- Review major decisions against the Four Governing Questions.
- Listen to collectors and industry participants without surrendering its principles.
- Revisit this Constitution as Cedriva grows.
- Hold leadership, products, partnerships, and community programs to the same standards.

## Article VIII — Amendments

This Constitution may be amended when experience reveals that it is incomplete, unclear, or no longer sufficient.

An amendment should include:

1. The language being changed.
2. The reason for the change.
3. The people or groups affected.
4. An evaluation against the Four Governing Questions.
5. The amendment date and approving authority.

No amendment should weaken Cedriva’s core purpose merely for short-term convenience or commercial advantage.

## Founder’s Declaration

We will never stop learning. We will never stop improving. We will never stop listening.
