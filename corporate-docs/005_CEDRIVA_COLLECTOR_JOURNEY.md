# Document 005 — The Cedriva Collector Journey

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Draft  
**Owner:** Brian Bowers  
**Created:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

Cedriva should never feel like the same app to every user.

The platform should evolve as the collector evolves.

---

## Level 1 — The Curious

> I’ve heard about premium cigars.

### Goals

- Learn the basics
- Understand terminology
- Feel comfortable
- Buy the first premium cigar

### Cedriva Provides

- Beginner Academy
- Cigar glossary
- How to cut
- How to light
- How to store cigars
- Strength guide
- Wrapper guide
- Factory guide
- Confidence

---

## Level 2 — The Explorer

> I’m trying different cigars.

### Goals

- Discover preferences
- Keep notes
- Remember favorites
- Understand manufacturers

### Cedriva Provides

- Personal journal
- Recommendations
- Flavor profile analysis
- Smoking history
- Ratings
- Wishlist
- Learning

---

## Level 3 — The Enthusiast

> I’m intentionally buying cigars.

### Goals

- Build a humidor
- Age cigars
- Understand releases
- Improve knowledge

### Cedriva Provides

- Inventory
- Humidor management
- Collection analytics
- Release calendar
- Price tracking
- Educational content

---

## Level 4 — The Collector

> I have a serious collection.

### Goals

- Protect assets
- Track rarity
- Organize inventory
- Find difficult releases

### Cedriva Provides

- Portfolio analytics
- Insurance reports
- Market values
- Investment dashboard
- Authentication
- Advanced AI
- Collection insights

---

## Level 5 — The Connoisseur

> I understand the industry.

### Goals

- Teach others
- Preserve history
- Share expertise
- Influence the community

### Cedriva Provides

- Verified Expert status
- Educational publishing
- Master reviews
- Historical archive
- Community leadership
- Mentorship

---

## Level 6 — The Legacy Collector

> My collection tells a story.

### Goals

- Preserve history
- Document provenance
- Pass collections to family
- Protect legacy

### Cedriva Provides

- Collection timeline
- Digital provenance
- Estate planning
- Historical archive
- Legacy profiles
- Permanent collector record

---

## Our Goal

Every collector should always feel like there is another level to reach.

The journey never ends.
