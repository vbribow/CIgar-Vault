# Document 002 — Cedriva Product Manifesto

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Working Draft  
**Owner:** Brian Bowers  
**Created:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

## Our Purpose

Cedriva exists to preserve, honor, and grow the culture of premium cigars.

We are building the world's most trusted platform for premium cigar collectors by making collecting more approachable, more intelligent, and more rewarding.

We are not simply building software.

We are building the future home of premium cigar collecting.

---

## Our Mission

To create the world's most trusted platform for premium cigar collectors while helping every enthusiast become a more knowledgeable collector.

---

## Our Vision

A world where every premium cigar has a story.

Every collector has a trusted home.

Every manufacturer has a direct connection to collectors.

Every retailer has better tools to educate customers.

Every lounge helps grow the culture.

Cedriva becomes the platform connecting them all.

---

## Our Promise

We will preserve the traditions that make premium cigars special while using technology to make the hobby more accessible, more educational, and more connected than ever before.

---

## We Believe

Every expert was once a beginner.

Knowledge should never intimidate.

Questions should always be welcomed.

Trust is earned through accuracy.

Community is more valuable than competition.

Collectors deserve world-class tools.

Tradition and innovation can strengthen each other.

---

## We Will Always

Serve collectors first.

Respect manufacturers.

Support retailers.

Celebrate lounges.

Preserve history.

Promote education.

Reward curiosity.

Protect authenticity.

Listen to our community.

Build for generations.

---

## We Will Never

Exploit collectors for engagement.

Promote misinformation.

Compromise authenticity.

Ignore the traditions of premium cigars.

Prioritize growth over trust.

Build features that do not improve the collector experience.

---

## Every Product Decision Must Answer

Does this educate?

Does this build trust?

Does this preserve history?

Does this strengthen the community?

Does this make collecting more enjoyable?

If the answer is no, we rethink it.

---

## Our Long-Term Goal

Cedriva becomes the global operating system for premium cigar collecting.

Not because we have the most features.

Because we have earned the most trust.

**Brian Bowers**
