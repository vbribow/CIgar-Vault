# Document 006 — The Cedriva Experience

**Version:** 1.1  
**Status:** Approved Draft  
**Owner:** Brian Bowers  
**Created:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

People do not remember software.

They remember how software made them feel.

Every interaction inside Cedriva should leave the collector feeling one or more of these emotions:

- Curious
- Confident
- Inspired
- Proud
- Connected
- Educated
- Welcomed
- Rewarded
- Accomplished
- Relaxed

---

## We Never Want Users to Feel

- Intimidated
- Confused
- Overwhelmed
- Judged
- Ignored
- Sold to
- Manipulated

---

## The Experience Should Feel Like

- Walking into the world’s greatest cigar lounge.
- Being welcomed by someone who genuinely wants to help.
- Having access to the knowledge of the world’s greatest collectors.
- Discovering something new every day.
- Building a collection with purpose.
- Sharing stories with friends.
- Preserving history.
- Feeling like you belong.

Founder, Cedriva
