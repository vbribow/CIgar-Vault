# Document 011 — Cedriva AI-First Philosophy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Draft  
**Owner:** Brian Bowers  
**Created:** July 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** July 23, 2026

Artificial intelligence should never replace the collector.

Artificial intelligence should elevate the collector.

Cedriva AI should feel like having the world’s greatest tobacconist available at any moment.

It should know:

- Your collection
- Your preferences
- Your budget
- Your smoking habits
- Your aging strategy
- Your favorite wrappers
- Your favorite factories
- Your favorite manufacturers
- Your collection goals
- Your wish list
- Your tasting history
- Your travel plans
- Your favorite lounges
- Your preferred retailers

---

Cedriva AI should answer questions before they are asked.

Recommend cigars before they are searched.

Identify patterns collectors cannot see.

Help collectors avoid mistakes.

Teach continuously.

Celebrate discoveries.

Preserve knowledge.

---

The AI should become more valuable every year the collector uses Cedriva.

Not because the AI changes.

Because it knows the collector better.

**Brian Bowers**  
Founder, Cedriva
